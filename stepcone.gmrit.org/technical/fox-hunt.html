FOX HUNT

Description

Fox hunt (also known as T-hunting, transmitter hunting, bunny hunting, and bunny chasing), is an activity wherein participants use radio direction finding techniques to locate one or more radio transmitters hidden within a designated search area. This activity is most popular among amateur radio enthusiasts, and one organized sport variation is known as amateur radio direction finding.So in this event the participants themselves should prepare their receivers and tune their receiver to transmitter's frequency to find out the hidden transmitter.
